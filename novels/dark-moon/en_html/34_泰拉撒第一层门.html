32 The First Gate of Terasha

The Terasha Record.

Within that structure, the little girl's existence was no longer blurred. Not human-shaped, not individual, but a kind of... stable information-stream cluster.

"You arrived faster than expected."

This time, her voice was not "spoken," but directly became his understanding. Jim did not answer, because he finally understood that language was for consciousnesses still needing to be bound inside bodies.

And now, he no longer needed it.

He could feel those trapped consciousnesses: Anna, Charlie, and the others... scattered across different layers, like fragments swept into different vortices.

"We begin now."

Before that sentence fell, in some distant layer, a huge dark shadow slowly turned over. It was like a whale, yet older than whales. A low-frequency echo traveled along the record layers. Jim did not know whether that was where Charlie was, nor whether it was the beginning of another story.

He only knew it was not an illusion.

Jim did not hesitate. If he still had "emotion" at this moment, it should have been an extremely simple certainty.

Not sacrifice, not courage, not even choice, only... he had finally stood in the correct position.

He thought he had understood many things: why he had been able to be extracted by the little girl from the Terasha Record, why that segment of information had chosen to remain, why the door on the dark side of the Moon had opened for him only once.

But in the next second, he understood again that those were only the first gate.

The true record was still unfolding deeper within.
